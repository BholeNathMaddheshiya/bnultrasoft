Personality Development :-

Personality : Personality is defined as a person's unique behaviors, unique emotions, and unique patterns of thought. The different people in this world has different personality.


Traits or Determinants of Personality :-

These determinants help explain why individuals differ in their personalities and why they respond differently to various situations.

⇛ What is self-awareness?

Self-awareness is the ability to focus on yourself and how your actions, thoughts, or emotions.

Self-awareness helps you to understand your strengths and weaknesses, and is a key driver of high performance at work.

  • ✬ Benefits of self-awareness:-
  • It helps us to become better decision-makers.
  • It gives us more self-confidence.
  • It decreases stress and makes us happier.

⇛ What is Motivation?

Motivation is the word derived from the word ’motive’ which means needs, desires, wants or drives within the individuals.

It is the process of stimulating people to actions to accomplish the goals. ( desire for money, success, recognition, job-satisfaction)

The direction of a motivational state is shaped by the goal it aims to achieve.


⇛ What is Self-discipline?

Self-discipline is the ability to control yourself and to make yourself work hard or behave in a particular way.

Self-discipline means self-control, It is one of the most important and useful skills to achieve success and everyone should possess this quality.


✬ Self Discipline Benefits and Importance

Self-discipline helps you to overcome the bad habits by meditating regularly. It gives you the ability not to give up after failure and setbacks, develop self-control, provide the ability to resist distractions, helps you to motivate yourself until you accomplish your goals.

✬ Self-esteem : Self-esteem is how we value and perceive ourselves. It's based on our opinions and beliefs about ourselves. Self-esteem is confidence in one's own worth, abilities, or morals.


✬ Self-efficacy : Self-efficacy is a person's belief in their ability to complete a task or achieve a goal. Self-efficacy is important because it plays a role in how you feel about yourself and whether or not you successfully achieve your goals in life.


✬ Self-motivation : Self-motivation is your ability to find enthusiasm, interest, and passion for a particular task, using them as the driving force to complete it. We all lose motivation from time to time. When you’re feeling unmotivated. So, make a working habit toward your goal , Track your progress.


✬ Time management : Time management is the process of planning and controlling how much time to spend on specific activities. Good time management enables an individual to complete more in a shorter period of time, lowers stress, and leads to career success.


✬ Stress Management : Stress is an automatic physical, mental and emotional response to a difficult event. It's a common part of everyone's life. Stress management skills can include exercise, relaxation, meditation, time management etc.


✬ Etiquettes & Manners : Manners are important to make a good impression on others in everyday life. They also helps you to feel good about yourself and your identity.


✬ Communication : Good communication skills are essential for personality development because it helps build strong relationships, convey trust and respect. To improve your communication skills, you should focus on understanding the needs of your audience. It is also important to take time to listen to each other and learn from each other, as it helps make sure that everyone has the same goals in mind.


✬ Report : A report is a document or a statement that presents information in an organized format for a specific audience and purpose. Although summaries of reports may be delivered orally, complete reports are usually given in the form of written documents.


✬ Presentation : Presentation is the practice of showing and explaining the content of a topic to an audience or learner.


✬ Interview skills : Interview skills are important to gain, just like the technical skills and workplace skills you need to perform well in a position.

  • Before the interview:
  • Self-preparation : Like resume, cover letter, and the details of any prior projects, if needed.
  • Research : Research like company reviews, its products and services, and its competitors—can help you determine if the career path will be a good fit.
  • During the interview:
  • Communication : Practice good communication in advance by observing your body language in front of a mirror. Ask a friend or family member to do a mock interview with you.
  • Introducing yourself : Your introduction at the beginning of an interview can help to set a positive tone for the rest of the conversation. After greeting your interviewer and thanking them for the chance to meet.
  • Presenting your qualifications : Once the interview is in motion, you’ll likely be answering a few behavioral or situational interview questions so that your interviewer can evaluate your qualifications.
  • Listening : Practice active listening so you can decide and better understand the interviewer questions and make eye contact as much as possible.
  • Asking questions : To practice this skill, write down your priority questions in advance of the interview and have them handy for quick reference.
